ABOUT US

BACKGROUND COMPANY

LegacyBooks Online, our online bookstore portal, is available 24/7 for the convenience of our shoppers. With ready-stocked titles, customers who order books via Legacy Books Online, from any part of Malaysia, will receive their orders within 3 working days. In addition to our fast delivery service, Legacy Books Online is the first ever online bookstore in Malaysia featuring a preview function. Customers have the option of previewing the contents or chapters of the books before deciding on their purchases. In addition, Legacy Books Online provides book-ordering services for customers, especially those who wish to purchase books only available overseas. Other features of this multi-lingual portal include new book recommendations, pre-order services, and exclusive promotions and giveaways.
